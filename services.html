Pick the level of help you actually need

We don't upsell. If a permit strategy doc is all you need, that's what you get. If you want a complete trip designed from scratch with support while you're out there, we do that too.

We tell you exactly how to get your permit — you apply yourself. We don't guide trips or file permits on your behalf. All services are planning and strategy only. You remain responsible for your permit application, safety decisions, and everything that happens on trail.

Order Now

You know where you want to go — you just can't figure out the permit system. This gets you a clear, zone-specific breakdown of how to actually get your permit: which lottery, which walk-up ranger station, which dates to target, and what the realistic odds look like.

What's included

Book a Blueprint

You've got dates and general fitness but no idea which area to target, which route makes sense, or how to navigate that zone's permit system. The Blueprint gives you a concrete starting point — region, entry zone, rough routing, and permit strategy — without designing every single day.

What's included

  • ✓
    30-minute intake call — we ask about fitness, experience, goals, flexibility on dates/location
  • ✓
    Region + zone recommendation — with reasoning, not just "go to the JMT"
  • ✓
    Rough route overview — entry/exit, approximate daily mileage, key passes and camps
  • ✓
    Permit strategy for your zone — same depth as Permit Strategy Only, included
  • ✓
    2 follow-up emails — clarifying questions after you receive the blueprint

Good fit if: You want a direction and a framework to fill in yourself. You're comfortable planning the details once someone hands you the right starting point.

Get a Custom Plan

A complete, ready-to-execute trip plan built around your specific dates, fitness, group, and goals. Every camp night is mapped. Every permit angle is covered. You get a curated gear list, not a generic checklist, and resupply logistics if your route is long enough to need them.

What's included

  • ✓
    Full day-by-day itinerary — mileage, elevation, camp locations, water sources, notes on each day
  • ✓
    Permit strategy — zone-specific, with backup options and walk-up advice
  • ✓
    Gear list — curated for Sierra conditions, your route, and the season. Not a generic backpacking list
  • ✓
    Resupply logistics — if your route requires a resupply, we'll map out exactly how to do it
  • ✓
    Bailout options — escape routes built into the plan, not an afterthought
  • ✓
    Two revision rounds — we refine the plan after your feedback

Good fit if: You want to show up with a plan you can trust. You're not looking to wing it — you want everything thought through in advance by someone who's been there.
