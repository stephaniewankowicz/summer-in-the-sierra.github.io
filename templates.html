Don't see your route? Request Custom

Templates are not personalized — Custom Itineraries are

Templates are built for a "typical competent hiker" on each route. They don't account for your specific fitness, group size, gear, or constraints. If you need something tailored to you, see the Custom Itinerary service.

Also: we tell you how to get your permit — you apply yourself. Permits are non-transferable and must be in your name. Templates include permit strategy for the route but do not secure any permit on your behalf.

Need something more specific?

Templates are great for well-traveled routes. For off-the-beaten-path goals or unusual constraints, a custom plan is worth it.
